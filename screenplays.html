Concealed Soul

The story of a young army deserter against the backdrop of the Iran-Iraq eight year war, hiding in a house, being cared for by an old man util his escape is arranged from the country. The narritive is about the deserter's strange relationship with the old man. Based on the short story by Majid Amini, Concealed Soul.

Sunset Drifters

The hilarious saga of four idealistic college students during one summer in the tumultous times of early 1960s America. While seraching for summer jobs they discover hard truths abouts life. Based on the novel by Majid Amini, Sunset Drifters.

The Afgan

An epic saga of a retired American Marine in search of his half-brother in Afganistan during the Soviet Union occupation, falling in love with an Afgan woman and his eventual involvement in the war.

Children of a Lesser Nation

The story of two highly-educated Iranians, taking the United States to the Internation Court of Justice for the atrocities of the 1953 coup directed by the CIA in thier mother country. Based on the novel by Majid Amini, Children of a Lesser Nation.

Joseph's Sorrow

The story of a Jewish Auschwitz survivor, set in South America years after World War II, searching for the German army captain who decided the fate of his parents by sending them to the gas chamber.

The Greatest Meeting

A story of the lives and the historical meeting two great men. The renowned religious scholar Mohammad Jallaledin Rumi and the legendary mystic Shams-e Tabrizi. Based on the novel by Majid Amini, The Greatest Meeting.
