The Howling Leopard is an enchanting tale of a seven-year-old boy, a German shepard, and a monstrously huge servant. The servant, a man with a flamboyant imagination, weaves fantastic war stories full of heroic and daring deeds in which he always plays a leading role. The young boy is captivated by the stories and the romantic figure the servant portrays, he worships and idolizes the man only to later find out he has been deceived. Upon this realization, a 'war' breaks out between the two and is maintained throughout the remainder of their relationship. It is a touching tale of love and betrayal, full of daring exploits, childhood pranks and the sweet and bitter pain of growing up.
