The Sunset Drifters is a delightful voyage of self-discovery and transformation of four young foreign students Marxists. Set in California during the 1960s, it narrates the hilarious saga of "the four musketeers", Cock-Eyed Jack, Cool-Hand Jeff, Fred the Magnificent, and their sole follower, tirelessly struggle to employ the disenchanted minorities, Blacks, American-Indians, and Mexican-Americans for the ultimate Proletariat Revolution in America.
